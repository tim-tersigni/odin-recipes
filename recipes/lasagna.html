Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of lasagna noodles (15 - 20 strips per lasagna)
  • 2 - 3 lbs. of ground beef
  • 1 lb. of scrambled hot italian sausage
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 - 3 cloves of chopped garlic
  • 2 lbs. ricotta cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • breadcrumbs
  • italian parsley
  • 0.5 lb. romano cheese, grated
  • 2.5 lb. mozzarella cheese, grated
  • salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until soft but not all they way cooked. Drain and soak in cold water until ready to build lasagna.
  2. Brown onions and garlic in a little olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef, sausage to oil. Salt and pepper to taste, then set aside.
  4. Mix all ingredients together, excluding romano and mozzarella
  5. Grease 9x15 stainless steel roasting pan. Put sauce on bottom of pan.
  6. Layer as follows:
    1. noodles
    2. sauce
    3. meat mixture
    4. sauce
    5. mozzerella and romano
    6. noodles
    7. sauce
    8. meat mixture
    9. sauce
    10. mozzerella and romano
    11. noodles
    12. sauce
    13. top with mozerella and romano
  7. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-60 min (may freeze before baking
