Sauce and Meatballs

Description

This is a family recipe for Italian sauce and meatballs. The sauce is tomato based with Italian sausage and roast. Don't forget your spaghetti and bread!

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/2 - 3/4 lb. salt pork
  • 6 links sausage (optional)
  • 3 - 5 lb. roast
  • 2 - 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3 large cans of tomato paste (12 oz. can)
  • 1 gallon can crushed tomato (6 lb. 9 oz.

Meatballs

  • 8 lb. hamburger
  • 4 - 5 cloves of garlic
  • parsley
  • 3 - 4 eggs
  • salt, pepper

Steps

Sauce

  1. Cut off salt pork
  2. Chop garlic (2 - 3 cloves) into salt pork until smooth.
  3. Cut roast into large chunks (salt and pepper to taste) and brown in melted salt pork.
  4. Add can of crushed tomatoes. Add little water to rinse out can.
  5. Add 3 cans tomato paste
  6. (Optional) Brown sausage in separate pan, add to pot.
  7. Simmer over low heat stirring occasionally until done. (All meat will be cooked through and oil will collect on top.)

Meatballs

  1. Cut garlic (4 - 5 cloves) into hamburger, add minced parsely.
  2. Add 3 or 4 whole eggs.
  3. Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Mix thoroughly by hand.
  5. Roll by hand into meatballs.
  6. Brown in pan and add to saucepot.
