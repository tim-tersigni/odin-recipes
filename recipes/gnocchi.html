Description

Here is a simple recipe for gnocchi. This is a soft, delicious potato pasta that goes well with a variety of sauces. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups instant potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • ricotta cheese
  • 2.5 - 3 lbs. of flour
  • 4 tblsp of butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Add just enough hot water to potatoes to moisten (about 2 cups).
  2. Add melted and cooled butter.
  3. Add 2 whole eggs.
  4. Add small handful of salt.
  5. Add 0.75 - 1 cup of ricotta cheese.
  6. Mix thoroughly by hand.
  7. Continue to work in additional flour to mixture until proper texture is achieved. (About 2.5 - 3 lbs. of flour.)
